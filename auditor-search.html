Boundary and provenance

Scope

This page is a public-record search surface. It is not an auditor recommendation engine and does not replace due diligence, licensing checks, independence review, certification-scope review, assessor scope review, or engagement-specific legal assessment.

See a buyer-facing example
Publication boundary

Only publication-safe fields are included: organisation identity, jurisdiction, source-specific public status markers, review or issue dates, visible public scope, stable source links, website links already exposed by the source, and refresh metadata. Records stay source-separate even when names overlap across registries.
